How it works

Ready to use in 48 hours

No downloads, no app store, no tech skills needed. Just a personalised app on your phone built specifically for your boat.

01

Fill in your boat details

Complete a short setup form with your vessel name, engine make, current hours and any maintenance history you have. Takes about 5 minutes.

02

We build your app

We build a personalised app pre-loaded with your vessel details, the correct service intervals for your engines, and your maintenance history baked in from day one.

03

Install on your phone

Open your unique link in Safari or Chrome and add it to your home screen. It works like a native app — no internet required once installed.

Features

Everything in one place

Built by a boat owner, for boat owners. Everything you need to stay on top of your vessel's maintenance — nothing you don't.

⚙️

Engine tracking

Port and starboard engines tracked side by side with correct service intervals for your specific engine make and model.

📊

Dashboard status

Red, amber and green status at a glance. See everything overdue, coming up, and all good — the moment you open the app.

📋

Maintenance log

Full history of every service with dates, hours, photos, notes and cost. Your boat's complete maintenance record in your pocket.

📦

Parts inventory

Track spare parts on board with part numbers and supplier details. Stock adjusts automatically when you log a task.

📅

Scheduled maintenance

Beyond engines — antifouling, batteries, seacocks, safety gear, generator and more. All your service schedules in one place.

📶

Works offline

Open it once on WiFi and it works fully offline at the marina, at anchor, or wherever you are. Log tasks anywhere, anytime.
